Summary of the Planets

  • Mercury - Closest Planet to the Sun, second hottest, made mostly of rock and metal.
  • Venus - Second Closest to the Sun, hottest, as well as the brightest planet.
  • Earth - Third planet, rocky, holds all life in the solar system.
  • Mars - last rocky planet, one third of the mass of Earth, humanities next destination.
  • Jupiter - Passed the Asteroid Belt, largest planet from the sun, has a big red spot.
  • Saturn - Has huge rings, less dense than water, second largest planet in the solar system.
  • Uranus - tilted 90 degrees, pronounced as "uruhnas".
  • Neptune - Last planet from the sun, named after the Roman God of the Sea.
